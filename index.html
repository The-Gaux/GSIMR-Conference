"Online International Conference 2025"

The New World of Work : Multidisciplinary Perspectives

The International Conference on 'The New World of Work: Multidisciplinary Perspectives' aims to bring together academicians, industry professionals, and researchers to explore emerging trends and challenges in various domains. This platform will facilitate knowledge exchange on topics ranging from global financial systems, HRM strategies, customer-centric operations, marketing automation, innovation, entrepreneurship, and AI-driven business models. Participants will have the opportunity to present their research, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and network with experts from diverse fields.



> Best Research Paper Award.

> Selected Papers will be published in Scopus/ ABDC/UGC Care Indexed Journals
