About Us

GSIMR is one of the Premier Business Schools in central India located in the heart of the city, Vijaynagar, Indore. Shri Govindram Seksaria Technological Society first setup SGSITS in the year 1956, It has been founded to strengthen the Engineering Education and later on looking onto the need of the hour, the society has also setup an independent Management Institute known as Govindram Seksaria Institute of Management and Research in 1997.





About Conference

The modern workplace is undergoing rapid transformation due to technological advancements globalization, and evolving workforce dynamics. This conference, "The New World of Work: Multidisciplinary Perspectives," aims to explore the challenges and opportunities arising from these changes. The objective is to provide valuable insights that help organizations and professionals adapt to the future of work effectively.
