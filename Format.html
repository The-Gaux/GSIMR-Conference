FORMAT FOR RESEARCH PAPER SUBMISSION


• Abstract Submission : Purpose, Design /Methodology/ Approach, Findings, Limitations Contributions, Keywords (up to six keywords) , Word Count: 200-250 words.
• Body:Times New Roman with 12 font size, single space and single column on standard A4 size paper.
• All heading must be bold-faced, sentence case, aligned left with heading 14 point-Times New Roman and sub-heading in 12 point.
• Manuscript length or word count : 6000 to 10,000 (Maximum 25 Pages) words in length. This includes all text, for example, the structured abstract, references, all text in tables, and figures and appendices Title Page Title, Author Name, Author Designation, Author Affiliation and Email-id, Corresponding author name for multiple authors.
• References : Should be in APA 7 style.
